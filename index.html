The product

The campus app allows access to a platform where students can exchange books and college materials, as well as objects. College students can also have a look have the menu, the sport or activies that are offered by the school and integrate the most important websites of the college. A Newsfeed is also available to fill the lack of communication methods typically felt by colleges. The app is ever evolving meaning that we use the licenses of all the colleges to provide new functionnalities and new technologies within all the college app.
